# Professional Self-Assessment

## Reflection on Educational Journey and Professional Growth

My journey through SNHU's Computer Science program has been transformative, significantly enhancing my technical skills and deepening my understanding of the computer science field. This program challenged me to engage with complex problems, develop efficient solutions, and adapt to a rapidly evolving tech landscape. By actively participating in diverse projects, I honed my skills in software development, data management, and system security, which prepared me for the practical demands of the tech industry and instilled a strong foundation for lifelong learning and professional growth.

## Contributions to Professional Goals and Employability

Throughout my academic career, I actively engaged in coursework and projects that not only reinforced fundamental computer science principles but also emphasized practical applications. For instance, in courses like "Foundation in Application Development" (IT 145) and "Programming Languages" (CS 210), I mastered object-oriented programming, which was crucial for developing structured and efficient software. My project in "Computational Graphics and Visualization" (CS 330), where I created a three-dimensional scene of my workspace using complex shapes (i.e., a mug, a laptop, a pencil, and a puzzle cube) using OpenGL, and my development of an Android app where users can track their weight and set weight goals in "Mobile Architecture and Programming" (CS 360) showcased my ability to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world applications. These experiences were instrumental in shaping my professional goals and enhancing my employability in fields requiring advanced technical competencies.

**Collaboration and Communication:** Throughout my coursework, particularly in the "Software Development Lifecycle" (CS 250) course, I gained theoretical knowledge of Agile methodologies, acting in various roles such as scrum master, product owner, tester, and developer. This experience allowed me to understand the dynamics of team collaboration and the importance of clear communication in project management. By simulating the Agile process and its different roles, I enhanced my ability to contribute effectively within team settings, ensuring that all project goals align with the overarching objectives and maintaining high standards of stakeholder engagement.

**Data Structures and Algorithms:** Through various courses, I honed my skills in understanding and implementing complex data structures and algorithms. Notably, in the "Advanced Programming Concepts" (CS 340) course, I developed an interactive dashboard for Grazioso Salvare using MongoDB which involved creating and manipulating data structures extensively. Furthermore, in the "DSA Analysis and Design" (CS 300) course, I explored sorting algorithms, linked lists, hash tables, and binary search trees. These experiences enhanced my ability to optimize data processing and solve complex computational problems effectively.

**Software Engineering and Database Management:** In "System Analysis and Design" (CS 255), I mastered UML diagrams and system design documentation, enhancing my software development skills and ability to translate complex requirements into clear development plans, as demonstrated in my work on the DriverPass application. In "Introduction to SQL" (DAD 220), I gained practical experience with relational databases using MySQL, focusing on data integrity and operational efficiency. Further expanding my skill set, "Full Stack Development" (CS 465) involved creating a comprehensive full-stack application using the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular, and Node.js). This project showcased my ability to integrate both backend and frontend development seamlessly, emphasizing scalable and robust software solutions.

**Security Practices:** In "Software Security" (CS 305), I honed my skills in secure coding, input validation, and vulnerability assessments, preparing me to develop robust solutions that ensure data privacy and security. I learned crucial security protocols, including the use of OWASP dependency checks and encryption algorithms, and gained practical experience in safeguarding client/server architectures. This course solidified my understanding of security within software development, emphasizing the anticipation of potential threats and the implementation of preventive measures to protect sensitive information.

## Integration of Artifacts and Demonstrating Capabilities

Returning our focus to the CS 499 Computer Science Capstone project, the strategic enhancements I applied to the Grazioso Salvare project clearly illustrate the comprehensive application of the skills developed throughout my coursework. Each enhancement was meticulously designed to meet specific technological and operational needs, showcasing my capability to integrate complex computer science principles effectively:

1. **Software Engineering and Design:** The implementation of the `RescueAnimalFactory` class significantly improved the system's modularity and scalability by centralizing object instantiation. This adjustment showcases my adeptness in software architecture and the application of design patterns to enhance software functionality.

2. **Algorithms and Data Structures:** I optimized the search functionality by introducing a hash map, which dramatically reduced the time complexity from O(n) to O(1) for key operations. This enhancement demonstrates my ability to efficiently handle data and optimize system performance through advanced data structures.

3. **Databases:** Transitioning the system to a robust SQLite database has enabled it to manage more complex interactions securely and efficiently. This development highlights my skills in database management and security, ensuring that the system can handle increased data loads without compromising on performance.

These enhancements not only underline my technical proficiency but also my strategic application of theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios, paving the way for detailed discussions on how they support my achievement of course outcomes.
